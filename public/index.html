FAQs

What is Next Hire?

Next Hire is a smart, easy-to-use resume builder designed to help individuals create professional and tailored resumes in minutes using industry-approved templates.

Do I need to pay to create a resume?

You can create and preview your resume for free. Some premium templates or features may require a small fee or subscription.

How do I start building my resume?

Click on "Create Resume", then follow the guided steps to enter your personal, educational, and professional details. Once done, choose a template and download your resume instantly.

Can I download my resume as a PDF?

Yes! Once you complete your resume, you can instantly download it as a high-quality, ATS-friendly PDF file.

Is this resume builder really free?

Yes, our basic resume builder is completely free to use. Premium templates and features are optional.

Can I edit my resume after saving it?

Absolutely! You can return anytime to update, revise, or improve your resume using our editor.

Are the templates ATS-friendly?

Yes, all our templates are designed to be Applicant Tracking System (ATS) friendly so your resume gets through automated filters.

Do I need to create an account to use this?

You can explore and build a resume without an account, but saving or downloading requires signing up for free.

Can I use the same resume for multiple jobs?

While you can, we recommend customizing your resume for each job to better match the job description and improve your chances.

What makes a good resume?

A great resume is clear, concise, highlights achievements, uses action verbs, and is tailored to the job you're applying for.

Can I add custom sections like Projects or Certifications?

Yes! You can add extra sections such as Projects, Certifications, Languages, or anything else that strengthens your profile.
